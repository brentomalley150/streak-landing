How it works

Simple for everyone involved.

Three people, three completely different experiences — all powered by the same streak engine.

1

Set up the challenge

Describe what you're working on in plain English. The AI builds the challenge — name, goal, duration, milestones — tailored to your group's size and skill level.

✦ AI pre-fills everything. You edit, not build from scratch.
2

Kids build their streak

One tap to log each day. A growing streak counter, earned badges, and a leaderboard that drives just the right amount of friendly competition.

✦ AI cheer is personalized to each kid's rank and next milestone.
3

Parents get real insights

Not just "4 out of 5 days." The AI synthesizes patterns: which days they're strongest, what their trajectory looks like, when they'll hit the next milestone.

✦ AI alerts admins when someone's at risk — before the streak breaks.
